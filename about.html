About the Festival

CIC Fest

Cameroon International Cultural Festival

The Cameroon International Cultural Festival UK (CICFESTUK) is a unique and vibrant cultural event established to bring together Cameroonians living across the United Kingdom in celebration of their rich and diverse cultural heritage.

Cameroon, often referred to as "Africa in Miniature," is home to over 250 ethnic groups, each with its own traditions, languages, music, dances, cuisines, attire, and customs.

Under the theme "Bridging Cultures, Celebrating Cameroon," CICFEST UK seeks to connect generations, communities, and cultures by creating a platform where Cameroonians and friends of Cameroon can celebrate, appreciate, and promote the richness of Cameroonian heritage.

Our Mission

CICFEST UK will serve as a platform to proudly showcase the beauty, diversity, and uniqueness of Cameroonian culture while fostering unity, collaboration, networking, and economic empowerment within the Cameroonian diaspora and the wider UK community.

The festival will create opportunities for cultural exchange, business promotion, talent development, and intergenerational learning, ensuring that Cameroon's rich traditions continue to thrive among future generations born and raised in the United Kingdom.
