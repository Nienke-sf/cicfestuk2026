About the festival

Celebrating Cameroonian Culture in the UK

CICFEST UK is the first Cameroonian International Cultural Festival to be held in the United Kingdom. Bringing together music, dance, food, fashion and heritage in one incredible day of celebration in Milton Keynes.

Find out more

Live Music

The finest acts in Afrobeats, Makossa, Bikutsi, and contemporary African music for an unforgettable day of live performance.

Food & Flavour

Savour the authentic tastes of Cameroon — from Ndolé and Eru to Suya and Puff Puff. A true culinary journey.

Community

More than a festival — a gathering of the Cameroonian diaspora, friends and allies united by culture.

Throughout the Day

🎭

Cultural Performances

Traditional music, dance and live entertainment from cultural groups across Cameroon — all on the central performance stage.

🥁

Grand Cultural Parade

A vibrant procession celebrating the rich heritage, rhythms and identity of Cameroonian communities.

🍲

Food Village

Taste traditional delicacies from different villages — Ndolé, Eru, Suya, Puff Puff and much more.

🛍️

Cultural Market

Browse and buy handmade crafts, traditional clothing, fabrics, jewellery, pottery, wood carvings and paintings.

🏆

Sports & Traditional Games

Tug-of-war, Sack races, Ludo, Draft, Dodge Game, Relay races and more fun traditional activities for all ages.

👶

Kids Area

A dedicated space for younger ones to play, explore and enjoy the festival safely.
